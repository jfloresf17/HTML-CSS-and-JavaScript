Spatial Data Science: Theory and Applications

In this section, we will learn fundamentals of Spatial Data Science.
Spatial Data Science is an interdisciplinary field about processes and systems to extract knowledge or insights as well as causal relationships of spatial phenomena from spatial and other data in various forms. On other hand, this science is focused equally on correlation and causality.
